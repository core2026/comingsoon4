What is Cedar Fever?

Is it a real fever? Usually no. It's an extreme allergic reaction to Mountain Cedar pollen that triggers inflammation, making you feel flu-like and fatigued.
Why is it so bad here? Texas Mountain Cedar is one of the only plants that pollinates in winter, releasing massive clouds that can travel hundreds of miles.
